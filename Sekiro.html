Sekiro: Shadows die twice

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is a 2019 action-adventure game developed by FromSoftware. Sekiro follows a shinobi known as Wolf, who attempts to take revenge on a samurai clan that imprisoned him and kidnapped his lord. The gameplay is focused on stealth, exploration, and combat, with a particular emphasis on boss battles.
