Elden Ring

Elden Ring is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware in collaboration with George R. R. Martin. Set in a dark fantasy world, Elden Ring promises a vast and interconnected landscape filled with danger and mystery.

Unleash your creativity and explore the open-world environment, filled with challenging enemies, hidden secrets, and epic boss battles. Elden Ring combines the masterful game design of FromSoftware with the rich storytelling of George R. R. Martin.

Prepare to embark on a journey that will test your skills, courage, and determination as you uncover the mysteries of the Elden Ring.
